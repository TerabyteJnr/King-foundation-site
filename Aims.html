Aims of the Kings Foundation

One of the main objectives of the Foundation is to promote the welfare of children in need due to poverty, disease or social injustice in addition to bring about a positive change in the social structure of the society for a better tomorrow. The Foundation seeks to attain the same through different channels such as:

1.Providing medicines, special diets and expert services from Doctors/Specialists.

2.Helping other Orphanages/Destitute Homes.

3.Providing financial assistance to the needy students to pursue their education.

4.Providing help to Nursery Schools situated in slums and poor localities.

5.Set up old age homes and to look after and assist persons who are unable to look after themselves due to poverty, disease and other infirmities.

6.Set up Hospitals, Clinics, etc. for providing medical facilities to the needy and poor.

7.Management & Support of orphanages, schools or other Educational institutions for children/young people. Such institutions must be run and managed in accordance recognized ethical principles, without any privilege or discrimination with regard to religion, caste or creed.
